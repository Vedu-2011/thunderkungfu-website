Teen & Adult

Sanda Kickboxing

Sanda is a practical combat system that combines striking, clinch work, and takedowns. It is one of the most effective and widely practiced combat sports in China and a legitimate self-defense skill.

Classes cover punches, kicks, sweeps, and throws in a safe and structured format. Appropriate for teens and adults of all fitness levels - no prior experience needed.

Tai Chi & Qigong

Traditional Tai Chi and Qigong combine slow, deliberate movement with breath control and mental focus. Long-term practice promotes blood circulation, reduces stress, and builds a calm, focused mind.

Ideal for anyone looking for a lower-impact approach to martial arts, or for students who want to complement their existing training with mindfulness and internal work.

Dragon-Lion Dance

Dragon and Lion Dance are among the most iconic cultural art forms in Chinese tradition. We teach this living heritage the right way - with proper technique, timing, and respect for its origins.

Students perform at community events, cultural festivals, and celebrations throughout the Bay Area. A one-of-a-kind experience that connects you directly to Chinese culture and heritage.
