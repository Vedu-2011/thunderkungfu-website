OUR STORY

More Than a Martial Arts School

Thunder Kung Fu Academy was founded in June 2015 to pass down authentic Chinese martial arts and culture to the next generation. Our instructors teach more than technique - they teach character, respect, and discipline.

Our 5,000+ sq ft training hall is fully equipped and we are proud members of the USA Wushu Kung Fu Federation.
