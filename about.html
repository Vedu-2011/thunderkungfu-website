About Us

WHO WE ARE

More Than a Martial Arts School

Thunder Kung Fu Academy opened its doors in June 2015 with one mission - to pass down authentic Chinese martial arts and culture to the next generation. The name itself reflects that. In Chinese, "Thunder" is the homonym of "Shangde," meaning morality - what to learn first, what to do first.

We believe martial arts positively changes lives. Not just through physical fitness, but through the discipline, respect, and character it builds in every student. Our instructors don't just teach how to fight - they teach how to live well.

Today we operate a 5,000+ sq ft fully equipped training hall in Dublin, CA, and serve students across the Bay Area. We are proud members of the USA Wushu Kung Fu Federation.
